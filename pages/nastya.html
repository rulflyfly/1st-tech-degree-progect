As a Child...

I used to be obsessed with America. My parents often watched "Friends" so I think that's where I got this feeling of sincere love towards the country I've never been to.

At the age of 19 I saved up my first 1000$ and flew to the US for my New Year. The biggest dream I had at that period of life came true.

Career Out of Nowhere

I liked learning English. It was just fun and nothing more. Somehow I couldn't find a better thing to do but learn new words and mimic the articulation of native English speakers in front of the mirror.

By the age of 15 I got a solid vocabulary and nice pronunciation. It was then that I got offered a job as a teacher. I never saw it coming.
